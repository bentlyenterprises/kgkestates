Welcome to the Kildrummy Glenkindie Estate

Welcome to the historic Glenkindie and Kildrummy Estates, nestled in the breathtaking heart of Aberdeenshire's picturesque countryside in Scotland. Rich with centuries of heritage and steeped in timeless charm, our estates offer visitors an authentic glimpse into Scotland's vibrant past and present. Explore the fascinating history of Kildrummy Castle and Glenkindie Castle, enjoy tranquil walks through the scenic woodlands, or simply escape to the serenity of our comfortable cottages. Experience the warmth and hospitality that have defined our estates for generations. We warmly invite you to discover, stay, and immerse yourself in this remarkable corner of Scotland.

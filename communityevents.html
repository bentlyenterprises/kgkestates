Community Events

The Kildrummy Glenkindie Estates continues the tradition of serving the community and the land through a variety of support. Learn more about our involvement, and how you can be a part of it.

The Kildrummy Veteran & Vintage Vehicles Club

The Kildrummy Veteran and Vintage Vehicles Club holds its annual Kildrummy Vintage Rally and Run on the second Sunday of June each year. It once again takes place at the original location where it all began on Kildrummy Castle Field on the Kildrummy Estate. Come join the rally.

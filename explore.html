Explore the Area

Glenkindie Estate Holiday Cottages sit in an inspirational landscape. they attract people with all kinds of interests. Visit the newly renovated Kildrummy Inn in Alford which offers some of the finest dining in Aberdeenshire. It has patio dining and a whisky snug.

The newly restored Forbes Arms Hotel and Riverside Restaurant offers a new dining experience, riverside restaurant and outdoor patio area overlooking the River Don. The Lounge Bar and the Snug offer a more relaxing place to eat and drink with luxurious furnishings throughout

The beautiful Dounside House has lovely gardens in which to stroll as well as a delightful afternoon tea service.

Leith Hall is a lovely estate house to tour with gardens to walk and a tea room.
